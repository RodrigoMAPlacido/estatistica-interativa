$$\bar{x}=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_i$$
$$\sigma=\sqrt{\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i-\mu)^2}{n}}$$
$$P(A\mid B)=\frac{P(A\cap B)}{P(B)}$$
$$E(X)=\sum_{i} x_i p_i$$
$$Z=\frac{X-\mu}{\sigma}$$
$$Var(X)=E(X^2)-[E(X)]^2$$
